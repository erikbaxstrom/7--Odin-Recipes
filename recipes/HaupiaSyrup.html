Haupia Syrup

Excellent on pancakes and waffles.

Ingredients

  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1 Tbs tapioca starch
  • 25 g palm sugar
  • 1/2 tsp Almond Extract
  • pinch salt

Instructions

  1. In saucepan, combine coconut milk, tapioca starch, and palm sugar. Bring to a simmer over medium heat, whisking frequently.
  2. After mixture comes to a gentle simmer, it will thicken slightly. Remove from heat, whisk well to remove any lumps, then add almond extract and salt.
  3. Store in refrigerator for up to a month
