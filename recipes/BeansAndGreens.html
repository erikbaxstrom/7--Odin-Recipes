Beans and Greens

A hearty meal.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz pasta
  • 1 qt chicken broth
  • 2 garlic cloves chopped
  • 1 lb hearty greens such as collards, kale, carrot tops etc
  • 1 can of beans
  • Garnish: Grated Parm, olive oil, lemon juice, fresh herbs, sour cream

Instructions

  1. Soak pasta in chicken broth
  2. Cook garlic in a large dutch oven in oil
  3. Add greens, beans, and most of the chicken broth and simmer over low heat until beans and greens are tender
  4. Add pasta and remaining broth. Cook 10 minutes or until pasta is done.
  5. Garnish with some or all of garnishes and enjoy!
